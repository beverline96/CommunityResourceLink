About Community Resource Link

Our Mission

Community Resource Link is dedicated to connecting community members with reliable, verified local services. We believe in building trust and strengthening communities by making it easy to find and access the services that matter most to you.

What We Do

We provide a platform where:

  • Service providers can showcase their expertise and reach more customers
  • Community members can easily discover verified, trusted services
  • Transactions are secure and transparent with M-Pesa integration
  • Feedback and ratings help build a trustworthy marketplace

Our Values

  • Trust: We verify all providers to ensure quality and reliability
  • Community: We support local economies and strengthen neighborhoods
  • Transparency: Clear pricing, honest reviews, and open communication
  • Accessibility: Making services easy to find and affordable for everyone
